Spaghetti Donut

Oh yeah! It's Spagonut time!

Looks can be decieving. Not in this case. Spagonut is the essential spaghetti donut.

You have to think about so much in your life. Sometimes you just want to eat something that you dont have to think about.
Enter Spagonut. It's a spaghetti donut. Dont think about it too much!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Sauce
  • "Binding agent""
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil.
  2. Cook pasta in pot.
  3. Strain pasta in the sink with a strainer.
  4. Stuff pasta in the pantented Spagonut mold.
  5. Pour sauce down the sauce spout.
  6. Put mold in the deep fryer for the length of Almost Cut My Hair by Crosby Stills and Nash
  7. Enjoy Spagonut!
